To cultivate presence,  
through the practice  
of mindfulness,
{: .centered}

is to open the mind  
to its natural state,  
a state of  
peace,
{: .centered}

a sea of  
possibility,
{: .centered}

where  
our authentic selves  
can express  
our creativity  
and our  
passion.
{: .centered}
