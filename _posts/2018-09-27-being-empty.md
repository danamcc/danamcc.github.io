So many times
throughout my life,
I have felt this emptiness,
wondering what was missing,
fearful of my lack.

Slowly,
I am seeing
that this emptiness
is the absence 
of all that holds me back,
that keeps me
from being myself.

I have never needed anything
beyond what I am.
