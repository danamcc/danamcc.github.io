When I am HERE
I don't know how old I am.
When I am HERE
I don't know what I've done.

When I'm HERE
I don't know what I should be doing.
When I'm HERE
I don't know what I should say.

When I'm HERE
there is an empty space before me,
right here,
where I am,
inviting me on.
