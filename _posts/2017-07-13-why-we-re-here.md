It's nice to believe
that we were sent here to learn.

Or, perhaps,
we simply landed here,
with the opportunity to learn.

To accept this gift,
whatever its source,
is to give our own lives meaning.

To reject it
is to ride the wheel of suffering,
hoping for redemption,
until we die.
