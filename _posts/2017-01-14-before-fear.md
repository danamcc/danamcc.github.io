In our direst dreams,
we descend into hell,
and in our sweetest,
we ascend to heaven.

But still,
as we dream,
buffeted by fear and hope,
we remain where we are,
not where imagination leads us,
but where dreams subside,
and we come to rest,
before hope,
and before fear.
