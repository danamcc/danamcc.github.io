_(with gratitude to William S. Burroughs)_

Language is a virus
from inner space
to other 
inner spaces.

How we speak
to our fellow humans
can infect each other's minds,
just as another virus
can infect our bodies.

Bad ideas
can kill;
good ideas
heal.
