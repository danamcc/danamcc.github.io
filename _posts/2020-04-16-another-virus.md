_(with gratitude to William S. Burroughs)_

Language is a virus
from inner space
to other 
inner spaces.

Our words
can infect the minds 
of our fellow humans,
just as another virus
can infect our bodies.

Bad ideas
may kill;
good ideas
may heal.
