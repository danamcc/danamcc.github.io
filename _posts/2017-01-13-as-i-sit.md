As I sit, 
empty,
I am what fills me.

I am the candle,
glowing in the dark,
I am the flickering flame,
I am the dancing shadow.

I am everywhere
and nowhere,
in the awareness 
flowing,
into what I see.

I am the seeer
and the seen,
and the space between.
