Goodbye.

It was a beautiful dream,
and it was a gift to share it.

Like most dreams,
it passed quickly
on awakening,
though the taste of it
still lingers,
drifting through memory.

Slipping sideways,
through the cracks
of consciousness,
it fades away,
a glimpse
of the possible.
