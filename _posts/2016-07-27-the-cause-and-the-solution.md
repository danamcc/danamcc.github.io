---
title: The cause and the solution
category: life
---

Everywhere we look,  
there is violence,  
anger,  
and hatred.  
  
And how   
is this virus spread?  
  
Through our words  
and actions.  
  
And what   
is the remedy?  
  
Infusing  
what we say and do  
with tolerance  
and compassion.  
  
We are the cause,  
and the solution.
