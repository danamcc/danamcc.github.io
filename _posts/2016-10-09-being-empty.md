---
title: Being Empty
category: life
---

When you are empty,  
there is room  
for the world   
to fill you,  
with its richness,  
grace, and beauty.  
  
There is no story   
to match this fulness,  
no thought  
that shines  
more brightly.  
  
To be nothing  
is to be a vessel  
for everything.
