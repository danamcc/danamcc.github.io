---
title: Who I am
category: life
published: true
---

Who I am  
is like a river flowing.  
  
What carried me here  
is just a stream   
of moments,  
now gone,  
that only live in story.  
  
Poised in this current,  
I drift ahead,  
propelled   
by the moment,  
into a sea  
which is not yet created,  
birthed in imagination.
