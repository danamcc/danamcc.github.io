---
title: Somewhere to be
category: life
published: true
---

There is nothing  
to become.  
  
To peal away  
the layers  
of thought and story,  
of fear and regret,  
is to reveal  
what was hidden,  
the being   
that always was,  
and always is.  
  
There is nowhere  
to go,  
only the discovery  
of where  
we already are.
