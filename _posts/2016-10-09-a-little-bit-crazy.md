---
title: A little bit crazy
category: life
---

Every human being  
is at least  
a little bit crazy.  
  
For most of us,  
there is a choice:  
  
listen to the voice,  
and follow it  
into craziness,  
or hear it  
for what it is:  
  
empty sounds  
whistling in the space  
of our greater minds.
