---
title: A little bit crazy
category: life
published: true
---

Every human being  
is at least  
a little bit crazy.  
  
For most of us,  
there is a choice:  
  
listen to the voices    
that drive us,  
and follow them    
into craziness,  
or hear them    
for what they are:  
  
empty sounds  
whistling in the space  
of our greater Mind.
