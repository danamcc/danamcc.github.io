Peace can easily be found
in tranquil waters,
if we allow the turbulence within
to be calmed 
by the peace around it.

Likewise,
there is peace to be found
in turbulent waters,
if we can only look beyond them,
to the sea of peace
that surrounds them
and us, and everything.
