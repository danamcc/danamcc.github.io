category: life

﻿A place of emptiness  
is a place to Be,  
without a plan,  
without regret,  
without boundaries.

It is the silence  
beyond “not enough”,  
the peace  
beyond trying.

It is the still point  
from which all paths start,  
a call to action,  
or to rest.
