I am tired of trying 
to be something:
a man, 
a person, 
a human.

I would rather be 
as I was born,
a blob of energy, 
always in flux,
forever undefined.

I would trade my identity
to become anonymous,
like a snowflake, 
to give up this need
to be anything 
in particular.
