The story of your life  
is only words.
{: .centered}

Erase them,  
and start anew!
{: .centered}

May this new chapter  
be an open book  
in which  
your most spontaneous  
and deeply felt wishes  
are written,  
bringing you  
ever closer  
to your truest being.
{: .centered}
